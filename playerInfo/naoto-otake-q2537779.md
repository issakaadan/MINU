Naoto Otake ( 大嶽 直人 , Ōtake Naoto ; born October 18, 1968) is a former Japanese football player and manager. He played for Japan national team . He was recently the manager [ 1 ] of J3 League club, FC Osaka . His younger brother Masato was also a footballer.
