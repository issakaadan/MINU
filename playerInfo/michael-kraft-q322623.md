Michael Willi Kraft (born 23 April 1966) is a German former professional footballer who played as a goalkeeper . [ 2 ] He played for 1. FC Köln , FC Gütersloh and FC Carl Zeiss Jena in Germany and Bakırköyspor in the Süper Lig and is currently the goalkeeping coach of Cambodia .
