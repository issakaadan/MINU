### English Introduction
Fernando Ruiz Hierro ( Spanish pronunciation: [feɾˈnando ˈʝero] ; born 23 March 1968) is a Spanish sporting director and former football player who played as a centre-back , sweeper or defensive midfielder .

He won five La Liga and three Champions League trophies with Real Madrid in 14 years after signing from Valladolid , during which he appeared in 601 official matches. [ 3 ] He also competed professionally in Qatar and England.

Hierro represented Spain on 89 occasions, appearing in four World Cups and two European Championships .
