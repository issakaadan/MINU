### English Introduction
Tomáš Huber (born 29 August 1985) is a former Czech professional football defender , who played for FK DAC 1904 Dunajská Streda .

In 2013, Huber, along with other Dunajská Streda players, was accused of accepting bribes for match fixing. [ 1 ] Huber denied involvement in the scheme. [ 2 ] For his role in the corruption scandal, Huber was banned from playing for 18 years. [ 3 ]
