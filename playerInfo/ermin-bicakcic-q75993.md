### English Introduction
Ermin Bičakčić ( pronounced [ěrmin bǐtʃaktʃitɕ] ; born 24 January 1990) is a Bosnian professional footballer who plays as a centre-back .

Bičakčić started his professional career at VfB Stuttgart , playing mainly in its reserve team , before joining Eintracht Braunschweig in 2012. Two years later, he moved to TSG Hoffenheim . In 2023, he came back to Eintracht Braunschweig. Three years later, he signed with Željezničar .

Bičakčić represented Germany and Bosnia and Herzegovina at youth levels, but decided to represent the latter at the senior level. He made his senior international debut in 2013, earning over 40 caps until 2025. Bičakčić represented the nation at their first ever major championship, the 2014 FIFA World Cup .
