### English Introduction
Takuya Takagi ( 高木 琢也 , Takagi Takuya ; born November 12, 1967) is a Japanese football manager and former player, who is currently the manager of J1 League club V-Varen Nagasaki , He played for Japan national team . He was a physical forward and the Japanese media and fans dubbed him as the Cannon of Asia .

His son Toshiya is also a footballer.
