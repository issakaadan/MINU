### English Introduction
Joan Capdevila Méndez ( Catalan pronunciation: [ʒuˈaŋ ˌkabdəˈβilə] ; born 3 February 1978) is a Spanish former professional footballer who played as a left-back .

Over 15 seasons in La Liga , he amassed totals of 410 matches and 36 goals, mainly for Deportivo and Villarreal . [ 3 ] In a 21-year senior career, he also played in Portugal, India, Belgium and Andorra.

Capdevila won 60 caps for Spain , and was part of the squads which won Euro 2008 and the 2010 World Cup .
