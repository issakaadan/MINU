### English Introduction
Gabriel Teodoro Martinelli Silva ( Brazilian Portuguese: [ɡabɾiˈɛw maʁtʃiˈnɛli] ; born 18 June 2001) is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a forward or left winger for Premier League club Arsenal and the Brazil national team .

Martinelli began his senior career playing for Ituano and signed for Arsenal in July 2019 for a reported fee of £6 million, where he has won the Premier League , FA Cup , and the FA Community Shield . He represented Brazil at the 2020 Summer Olympics , winning an Olympic Gold in men's football . Martinelli made his senior debut in 2022 and has played at the 2022 FIFA World Cup , 2024 Copa América and 2026 World Cup .
