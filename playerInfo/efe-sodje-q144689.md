### English Introduction
Efetobore Peter " Efe " Sodje (born 5 October 1972) is a former professional footballer who played as a centre-back . He played for several Football League clubs, and is famous for wearing a do-rag , with the headband often having the words "Against All Odds". Born in England, he represented Nigeria in the 2000 African Cup of Nations and the 2002 World Cup . [ 3 ]

Sodje began his career at Stevenage Borough and later played for Luton Town , Colchester United , Crewe Alexandra , Huddersfield Town , Yeovil Town , Southend United , Gillingham , Bury , Barrow and Macclesfield Town in two spells.
