### English Introduction
Lazar Marković ( Serbian Cyrillic : Лазар Марковић , pronounced [lâzaːr mǎːrkoʋitɕ] ; born 2 March 1994) is a Serbian professional footballer who plays as a winger . He is currently without a club following his departure from Apollon Limassol at the end of his contract.

He started his career at Partizan before moving to Benfica in 2013, where he won a domestic treble in his only season, after which he completed a £20 million move to Liverpool. He spent most of his time out on loan, at Fenerbahçe , Sporting CP , Hull City , Anderlecht and Fulham .

Marković made his full international debut for Serbia in 2012, going on to earn 22 caps for the national team. [ 5 ]
