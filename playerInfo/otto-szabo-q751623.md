### English Introduction
Otto Szabó ( Hungarian : Szabó Ottó ; born 1 March 1981) is a Slovak football defender of Hungarian ethnicity . He currently plays for the club FK DAC 1904 Dunajská Streda .

Szabó played first international match for Slovakia against San Marino on 13 October 2007.
