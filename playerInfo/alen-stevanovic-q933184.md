### English Introduction
Alen Stevanović ( Serbian Cyrillic : Ален Стевановић , pronounced [steʋǎːnoʋitɕ] ; [ 1 ] born 7 January 1991) is a professional footballer who plays as a winger . Born in Switzerland, he represented Serbia at international level.

A product of the Radnički Obrenovac youth academy, Stevanović made name for himself while playing for Torino . He made his debut with the Serbia national team in 2012.
