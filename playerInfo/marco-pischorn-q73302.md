### English Introduction
Marco Pischorn (born 1 January 1986 in Mühlacker ) is a German retired football defender .

In October 2007, he was promoted to the first team of VfB Stuttgart . [ 1 ] He had his professional debut on 27 October 2007 against Bayer Leverkusen . [ 2 ] He left for SV Sandhausen in 2010, where he would spend three and a half years before joining Preußen Münster .
