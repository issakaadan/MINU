### English Introduction
Kagisho Evidence Dikgacoi / d iː ˈ x ʌ ʃ w ɑː / [ 2 ] (born 24 November 1984) is a South African retired professional footballer who is currently the manager of Witbank Spurs . [ 3 ] He represented South Africa at international level.

He made his international debut in 2007, and since then has earned 54 caps, scoring twice. He played at the Africa Cup of Nations in 2008 and 2013, and at the 2010 FIFA World Cup .
